Automated Blood Bag Sealer

Designed and fabricated an automated blood bag sealer using an Arduino, microservos, and custom wiring on a Screw Terminal Proto Shield. The system uses a Logic Level MOSFET to control the sealer motor sequence for full digital control. Mechanical and Thermal Analysis was performed in order to validate gear ratios and heating of Blood Bag Components.
